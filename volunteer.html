VOLUNTEER WITH US

Volunteers are what keeps Fællesskabet I København running and we rely on volunteers to help maintain the fridges, collect food from donors, build the project and help us continue fighting food waste and food insecurity.

Anyone can volunteer with us and volunteer shifts are coordinated through the open source platform Karrot. It is developed uniquely for this type of project by members of Foodsavers Worldwide and Foodsharing Germany.
